📊 Grading Standards

Every unit passes a certified 100-point inspection in our Dubai facility. Grading report signed by technician and included with every shipment.

A-GRADEPremium — Like New Condition
  • ✅ Battery health ≥ 80% — tested with HWiNFO64
  • ✅ No cracks, no broken plastic — cosmetically clean
  • ✅ Screen: no dead pixels, no discolouration, no cracks
  • ✅ All ports working: USB, HDMI, headphone, charging
  • ✅ Keyboard & trackpad fully functional — no missing keys
  • ✅ Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, camera, microphone all tested
  • ✅ 12-month replacement warranty
B-GRADEGood — Light Cosmetic Wear
  • 🟡 Battery health 60–79%
  • 🟡 Visible scratches on lid or base — no cracks
  • ✅ All ports & hardware fully functional
  • ✅ Keyboard & trackpad work perfectly
  • 🟡 6-month parts warranty
C-GRADEFunctional — Value Price
  • 🟠 Battery health below 60%
  • 🟠 Visible cosmetic damage — no structural cracks
  • ✅ Core functions working: boots, display, keyboard, Wi-Fi
  • 🟠 3-month DOA warranty
📎 Each unit ships with a grading report, technician test sheet & serial number documentation.
